Privacy Policy – VespaRadar

VespaRadar is an application designed for monitoring apiaries and reporting sightings related to the invasive species Vespa velutina.

Data Collection

The application may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Precise location data (latitude and longitude), provided when creating reports, traps, or observations
  • User-provided content such as observations, notes, and trap data
  • Account information such as email address (when authentication is used)
  • Technical identifiers necessary for the functioning of the application

Use of Data

The collected data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide core application functionality (reporting, visualization, and management of apiary data)
  • To calculate and display wasp pressure indicators
  • To improve system performance and reliability

Data Sharing

Data is not sold or shared with third-party companies.

User-generated content, including geolocated reports and observations, may be visible to other authorized users within the system for operational and environmental monitoring purposes.

Data Storage

Data may be stored locally on the user's device and/or on secure servers associated with the application.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill its purposes. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user data. All data transmitted between the app and servers is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

User Rights

  • Request access to their data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their account and associated data

Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion at any time via the following page:

https://colmenapro.github.io/ColmenaPro-Web/eliminacion-cuenta.html

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the application implies acceptance of any changes.
